OPFL Scoring

6 points for TDs and TD passes, 2 points for a 2 pt. conversion. Points cannot be less than zero. Special teams TDs count as TDs for the individual player.

Passing:
200 yards = 2 pts.
1 point for every 50 yards passing thereafter.  
(Below 200 yards means no points).
Minus 1 pt. for each INT.
Minus 3 pts. for each INT returned for TD.
Minus 1 pt. for every fumble lost
Minus 3 pts. for every fumble lost returned for TD.

Rushing/Receiving:
75 yards = 2 pts.
1 point for every 25 yards rushing/receiving thereafter.
(Below 75 yards each category 0 no points).
Minus 1 pt. for every fumble lost, interceptions thrown.
Minus 3 pts total for every fumble lost, interceptions thrown, returned for TD.

Alternate Combined Rushing/Receiving Yards Bonus:
For RBs/WRs: 100 combined rushing/receiving yards = 2 pts, plus 1 bonus point for every 25 combined yards thereafter. No point ceiling.
For TEs: 75 combined rushing/receiving yards = 2 pts, plus 1 bonus point for every 25 combined yards thereafter. No point ceiling.

NOTE: This is alternate, not additional, scoring for these positions. Player gets whichever bonus yields more points in any game.

TE Scoring:
50 yards = 2 pts.
1 bonus pt. for every 25 yards rushing/receiving thereafter.
(Below 50 yards means no points).
Minus 1 pt. for every fumble lost, interceptions thrown.
Minus 3 pts. for every fumble lost, interceptions thrown, returned for TD.

Kicker scoring:
PAT = 1 pt.  
Up to 29 yard FG = 1 pt.
30-39 yard FG = 2 pts.
40-49 yard FG = 3 pts.
50+ yard FG = 4 pts.
Missed/blocked FG = minus 2 pts.
Missed/blocked PAT = minus 1 pt.

Defense: 
 
Interception = 2 pts.
Fumble recovery = 2 pts.
Safety = 2 pts. 
Blocked punt or FG = 2 pts.
Blocked PAT = 1 pt.
Touchdown = 4 pts.
Sack = 1 pt.

Points Allowed:
Shutout = 8 pts.
2-9 points yielded = 6 pts.
10-13 points = 4 pts.
14-17 points = 2 pts.

28-31 points yielded = Minus 2 pts.
32-35 points = Minus 4 pts.
36 + points = Minus 6 pts.

* TDs count on INTs, fumble recoveries, and blocked punts or placekicks but not on punt/kickoff returns.

HC
4 points for a home favorite win (using ESPN spread).
5 points for a home underdog win.
6 points for a road favorite win.
7 points for a road underdog win.
